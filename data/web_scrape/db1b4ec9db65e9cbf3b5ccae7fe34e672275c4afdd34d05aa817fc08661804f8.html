How To Find Your Lens Code

All Lewmar Hatches and Portlights (except Old Standard) produced since October 1997 have identification information stamped on the acrylic. On Portlights and Fixedlights the information is under the LEWMAR branding. On hatches the information is between the right hand handle and the edge of the acrylic.

Important Information

Hatches Produced after October 1997 have information stamped in the acrylic which will identify them – please quote this when ordering spares.
